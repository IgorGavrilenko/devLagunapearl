Necklace Lengths

Our exquisite collection of pearl necklaces includes classic Freshwater, lustrous Akoya, and exotic and South Sea strands. Our wide selection of pearl grades, shapes and colors will ensure you find the perfect piece.

Choker Length

This necklace length is referred to as the "classic necklace" size and typically sits at the hollow of her neck above the collar bone.

Princess Length

One of the most popular length selected by women today, the princess length is intended to sit at or right below the collar bone. Its length creates versatility and is well suited with a variety of women's attire, including V-neck shirts and button-up blouses.

Matinee Length

The pearl matinee necklace is a great choice for casual or business attire. It typically sits well below the neckline and is desired because of its versatility as a casual necklace, as well as a necklace for formal occasions. The matinee length also goes well as a multi-layered necklace, with a shorter choker or princess length sitting above it.

Opera Length

This pearl length is popular for its versatility as one long necklace or the ability to wrap around a woman's neck twice, creating a double strand of pearls. This necklace length is also very suitable for layering and its ability to be appropriate in both casual and formal settings.

Rope Length

Any necklace longer than Opera Length is typically considered a "pearl rope" and can be designed to create maximum versatility for the wearer, such as one long necklace, two layered necklaces, or three shorter necklaces when wrapped around the neck multiple times.
