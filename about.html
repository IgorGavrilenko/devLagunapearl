  • About Us

  • Laguna Pearl was created as a result of the public demand for high quality pearl jewelry at competitive pricing.

    OUR about

  • With our roots as a wholesale pearl jewelry operation, our organization has striven to cut out the middle man and sell directly to the retail jewelry consumer. As opposed to selling pearl jewelry products to retail jewelers who mark the prices up 2 to 3 times their cost, we have built our online business by passing on the savings directly to the consumer.

    From our inception, Laguna Pearl has continually thrived on providing high quality pearl jewelry products with the best customer service on the internet.

    Our ability to customize any product on our website as well as be the leading source for custom orders of pearl jewelry on the internet is a testament to our vast platform and hiabout of expertise.
